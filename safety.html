Women Safety & Legal Rights

Know your rights, stay informed, and protect yourself with these tips and laws.

Quick Safety Tips

Share Location

Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone at night.

Emergency Numbers

Memorize quick dial numbers like 112 (Emergency), 1091 (Women Helpline).

Stay Alert

Avoid using headphones in isolated areas and keep pepper spray handy.

Know Your Legal Rights

Right to Free Legal Aid

Every woman has the right to free legal aid under the Legal Services Authorities Act, 1987.
